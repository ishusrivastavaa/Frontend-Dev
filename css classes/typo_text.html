The Art of Typography

Typography is the visual art of arranging type to make written language readable and appealing. It involves choosing the right font family, setting appropriate line spacing, and balancing text alignment to enhance the reader’s experience.

Different font weights such as light, regular, and bold help create hierarchy and emphasis. Similarly, font size and letter spacing play a crucial role in improving text clarity and rhythm.

In digital design, typography combines creativity with structure. The first letter or the first line can often capture a reader’s attention when styled uniquely — making a page feel elegant, inviting, and well-crafted.
